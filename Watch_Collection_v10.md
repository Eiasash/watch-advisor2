## Collection Philosophy

### Movement Duo (Grand Seiko)
| Movement Type | Watch | Ref | Philosophy |
|---------------|-------|-----|-----------|
| Spring Drive | Snowflake | SBGA211 | Glide motion — hybrid quartz/mechanical |
| Hi-Beat 36000 | Rikka | SBGH351 | Precision — 10 beats/sec |

### Genuine vs Replica Strategy
- **Genuine** for respected but understated brands (Grand Seiko, Cartier, JLC, Tudor, TAG, Omega, German makers)
- **Replica** for high-impact dial colors that cost ₪100K+ authentic (turquoise Day-Date, purple OP, burgundy VC, green AP)
- **Zero redundancy** — each piece fills a specific role

### Context Rotation Rules
| Context | Genuine Preference | Replica Safe | Notes |
|---------|-------------------|-------------|-------|
| Clinic | Strong | Santos 35mm only | Credibility matters |
| Formal | Required | — | Reverso, Pasha, Santos |
| Smart Casual | Preferred | Acceptable | Match formality |
| Date Night | Either | Great for color | Impact > credibility |
| Riviera/Summer | Either | Ideal for bold | Day-Date Turq, IWC Ingenieur |
| Weekend/Casual | Either | Perfect | Low stakes |
| Flex | Either | Statement colors | Maximum visual impact |

### Strap-Shoe Rules (Mandatory)
- Brown leather strap → Brown Ecco lace-ups
- Black leather strap → Formal shoes or white sneakers
- Metal bracelet → Any footwear (rule exempt)
- NATO/canvas/rubber → White sneakers preferred
- **No loafers ever**
